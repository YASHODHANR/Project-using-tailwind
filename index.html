Mahakumbh

The journey to the Mahakumbh was a spiritual and awe-inspiring experience. The grand spectacle, colorful processions, and spiritual discourses were truly remarkable.

Prayagraj

  • One of the oldest and most significant religious cities in India.
  • Home to the Triveni Sangam, where the Ganga, Yamuna, and Saraswati rivers meet.
  • The Kumbh Mela, held every 12 years, draws millions of pilgrims.
  • Visit the historic Allahabad Fort built by Emperor Akbar.
  • Explore Anand Bhavan, the ancestral home of the Nehru family.

Varanasi

  • One of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world.
  • Famous for its ghats along the Ganges River and the grand Ganga Aarti.
  • Experience the mesmerizing sunrise boat ride on the Ganges.
  • Discover the rich heritage at the Banaras Hindu University.
  • Visit the Kashi Vishwanath Temple, an iconic pilgrimage site.

Ayodhya

  • Birthplace of Lord Rama and an important religious city.
  • Home to the Ram Janmabhoomi and several ancient temples.
  • Witness the beautiful Saryu River and its evening aarti.
  • Explore Hanuman Garhi, an important temple dedicated to Lord Hanuman.
